# Sidebar AI — Pricing & Service Tiers

## How We Compare to Harvey AI

Harvey charges **$1,200 per attorney per month** for legal case work (research, briefs, contracts).

We charge a **flat monthly fee** — regardless of firm size — for everything Harvey doesn't cover: intake, billing, emails, marketing, meeting notes, and operations.

| | Harvey AI | Sidebar AI (Full Integration) |
|---|---|---|
| What it covers | Case work | Operations |
| Pricing model | Per attorney/month | Flat monthly fee |
| 5-attorney firm | $6,000/mo ($72K/yr) | $3,750/mo ($48.5K/yr) |
| 10-attorney firm | $12,000/mo ($144K/yr) | $3,750/mo ($48.5K/yr) |
| 20-attorney firm | $24,000/mo ($288K/yr) | $3,750/mo ($48.5K/yr) |
| Minimum seats | ~20 | No minimum |
| Setup/training | Not included | Included ($3,500 one-time) |

**The bigger the firm, the better our value.** Harvey scales per seat. We don't.

## Sales Script Cheat Sheet

### Pricing Pitch

> "Setup is $3,500 — that covers the full audit, all your templates, Zoom AI, voice matching, and team training. After that, you choose how involved you want me to be. Starter is $1,250 a month for light check-ins and support. Growth is $2,250 for firms that want me more hands-on with regular audits. Full Integration is $3,750 — I'm your AI operations partner for the full year, working directly with every person on your team."

### Harvey Comparison Pitch

> "Harvey charges $1,200 per attorney per month — and it only covers case work. For a 10-person firm, that's $144,000 a year. I charge $3,750 a month flat — regardless of firm size — and I cover everything Harvey doesn't: intake, billing, emails, marketing, meeting notes. That's 66% less, and we handle the entire operations side."

### Upselling from Starter to Growth

> "For $1,000 more a month, I come back at the 3-month mark and do a full re-audit of your workflows. Firms always discover new tasks by then. You also get 24-hour support and I refresh your firm's writing voice every quarter so Claude keeps sounding like you."

### Upselling from Growth to Full Integration

> "For $1,500 more a month, I'm fully embedded. Same-day support, 10 templates a month, and I sit down with each person on your staff every quarter to make sure they're getting the most out of the system. That's the difference between having AI tools and your whole firm actually using them every day."

### ROI Framing

> "Your team is losing 5+ hours a day to admin work that AI handles in seconds. At your billing rates, that's $15,000-$25,000 a month in recovered capacity. My fee pays for itself in the first week — every month after that is pure upside."

### Objection: "That's expensive"

> "A part-time paralegal costs $3,500-$4,500 a month and handles tasks for one attorney. I set up a system that handles those same tasks for your entire firm — every role, every person — and it keeps getting better every month. And I'm still a fraction of what Harvey charges."

### Objection: "We'll start with Starter and upgrade later"

> "Totally fine. Just know that the first 6 months is when your team builds the habits. That's when having more support makes the biggest difference. But Starter is a great starting point."

### Objection: "We'll just figure it out ourselves"

> "You could. But most firms try for a month, get frustrated, and stop. The difference is having someone who's done this before — who knows exactly which tasks to automate, builds the templates for you, and makes sure your team actually uses them every day."
